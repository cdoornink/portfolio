Recipes
Family meal planning and shopping list
December 2015

I developed this purely for my wife and myself to use. It has all the recipes that we have collected other sources, normalized and simplified for ease of use. When making a meal plan for the week, we simply add the meals we want and the appropriate ingredients are added to the shopping list, organized by aisle. Other items can easily be added to the shopping list and everything stays in sync no matter who is adding things.

This is an Ember app integrated with Firebase. Many friends have asked if I can make it available for their use which would require a lot of changes and added authentication features which I might eventually when I have the time.
